Privacy Policy

Welcome to Color Switch Run. Your privacy is important to us.

1. Data Collection

Color Switch Run does not collect any personally identifiable information. We do not track your location, contacts, or personal data.

The game stores your high score locally on your device. This data is not shared with any external servers.

This version of the game does not contain third-party advertisements or analytics tracking.

Our game is safe for all ages and does not collect information from children.
